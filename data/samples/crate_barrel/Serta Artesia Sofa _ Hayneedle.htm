Description

Enjoy comfortable lounging in smaller spaces with the Serta Artesia Sofa presented in a modern mid-century design. This stylish sofa seats three comfortably and features a tufted back for a complementary look. Choose from the assorted available colors to find the look that suits your taste, and enjoy a durable sofa that's simple to maintain thanks to the microfiber upholstery. The hardwood frame gives you plenty of support, and it includes two matching pillows for even more comfort.

We know how important home is, so we want to help you find exactly what you love for where you live. That's why we offer complimentary swatches for you to see and touch. Seeing a swatch in various light and placement, and with your existing decor, can help you make the perfect choice. To order a free swatch, please call 888.880.4884.
About Serta

From their Pillow Soft mattress introduced in 1970 to the Perfect Day Line introduced in 2005, Serta has consistently made investments in innovative features to ensure the comfort and durability of every mattress they sell. From the beginning, Serta licensees recognized the value of a strong national brand, and they had a product that stood for innovation and quality. One licensee invented the industry's first tuftless innerspring mattress. Serta's founders named it the Perfect Sleeper, and it remains the best-selling mattress in America.

Great couch for the price. The color is a beautiful neutral grey. Cushions are extremely cushy (you sit and sink in quite a bit) and they only Velcro on the outside edge to secure to the sofa. The way the arms attach would make it easy to break if you put the wrong kind of pressure against them, or have a wild 3 year old that likes to climb on the arms of furniture. This sofa is in our front living room and is a great affordable addition to a sitting area - we just make sure our toddler doesn't climb or hang on the arms!
